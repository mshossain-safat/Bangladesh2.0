Dr. Muhammad Yunus, a Bangladeshi social entrepreneur and economist, is renowned as the pioneer of microfinance and microcredit. He founded the Grameen Bank, which provides small loans to the poor, empowering them to break free from poverty. Awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006, his innovative work revolutionized global poverty alleviation efforts. Dr. Yunus is also a strong advocate for social business and sustainable development.
